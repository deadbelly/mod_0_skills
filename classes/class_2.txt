Class:
Plate

Attributes:
isClean (boolean)
heatLevel (integer- this restaurant uses a scale of 10)
weightOfFood (integer)
itemsOnPlate (array of strings)

Methods:
clean (sets isClean to true)
letCool (decreases heatLevel)
addFood (adds one or more strings to itemsOnPlate and increases weightOfFood)
removeFood (removes one or more strings to itemsOnPlate and decreases weightOfFood)
